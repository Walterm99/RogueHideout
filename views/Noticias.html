Ya dio inicio la segunda temporada del Mythic Dungeon International


    Season 2 of the Mythic Dungeon International has begun its Time Trial stage! Teams will push keys starting at level 16, up to a maximum level of 21 and competing for the fastest times in Halls of Atonement and De Other Side.


Time Trials Have Begun


    The Mythic Dungeon International Season 2 has begun with the Time Trials! These will last until Tuesday, August 24th at 10:00 AM Pacific time and take place on the Tournament Realm.


   Teams start with level 16 keystones for Halls of Atonement and De Other Side and can push these keys up to a maximum of level 21, at which point they can increase their score further by completing the keystone faster. These keystones have the following affixes:

    Halls of Atonement:

  • Tiránica , Detonante , Volcánica , Atormentado

    • De Other Side:

  • Reforzada , Furibunda , Explosiva , Atormentado


   The top 24 teams will advance to the Group stage! For more information on the MDI Rules, check out the tournament on Gamebattles.


Mythic Dungeon International Season 2

   MDI Season 2 has a slightly different format with 24 teams advancing to the Group Stage. These 24 teams will be split into 3 groups, and 2 teams will advance from each group. 1 team will also advance from China and the Last Stand tournament.
