Tips de la semana: Evitar el sangrado del ejecutor Varruth

This Mythic+ tip of the week revolves around Verdugo Varruth, one of the lieutenants from the Atormentado seasonal affix. There is a way to avoid the damage from his Carnicería cast if you have the right tools.

Tabla de contenidos Dodging Executioner Varruth Threat Drop Ability Dodge / Parry Why Should You Do This? Potential Other Abilities Dodging Executioner Varruth Threat Drop Ability One way to avoid the damage from Carnicería is to be a Rogue, a Hunter, a Mage, or a Night Elf and use a form of combat drop while he's mid charge. This allows you to mitigate the damage at the trade of your ability. These abilities are: Esfumarse for Rogues Fingir muerte for Hunters Invisibilidad for Mages Fusión de las sombras for Night Elves Once his cast is finished and he has started moving, you are clear to use these abilities. Note that for mages, you will need to use Invisibility once he targets you and make some space, since it takes a few seconds to activate. Dodge / Parry Another way to dodge Executioner Varruth's Carnicería is to use any ability which allows players to Dodge or Parry attacks. This includes Muerte a espada from Warriors and Evasión for Rogues. Why Should You Do This? In addition to the healing debuff caused by Varruth in the form of Tanatofobia making it hard to catch up from the damage taken, this damage will eventually become unhealable. Whether that is in the form of a one shot or your healer not being able to top you before the next charge comes out, being able to dodge this successfully will be very important. Potential Other Abilities While I don't have confirmation on any of these working yet, I have been told by a few people that there are a few more abilities that you can do this with. The caveat is that they require you to be very far away from Varruth in order to make them work, so while it can be done it may not be worth the time or effort until the ability is one shotting you. The speculated abilities are Traslación (and all other forms of the ability, such as Night Fae's Forma de alma, Warlock's Círculo demoníaco: Teletransporte, etc.) and Venthyr's Puerta de las Sombras. If you find yourself able to test these and they do work, please let me know!
